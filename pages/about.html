The Story Behind
Transformation HQ

Transformation HQ was born from a simple belief — that every person, regardless of their starting point, has the potential for a radical physical and mental transformation. Our founder, Coach Aakash Verma, had that transformation himself.

After losing 30 kg and rebuilding his body from scratch, Aakash realised the fitness industry was failing people. Cookie-cutter plans. Impersonal service. No real accountability. He built THQ to fix that.

Eight years, 2,000+ transformed members, and 15 certified trainers later — we're just getting started.
